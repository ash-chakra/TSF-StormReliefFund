About Us

The Sparks Foundation started their Storm Relief Fund as a gesture to donate back to the Indian States which are badly affected by storms. Storm is a natural disaster and it can happen anywhere. The Sparks Foundation Storm Relief Fund collects fund for Indian people who are residing in the storm hit areas of a particular Indian State. The fund collected by common people will be utilized to give food,shelter and clothes to the people badly affected by storms.
